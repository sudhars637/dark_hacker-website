About us


SBP travels founded in 2022 by Sudharshan ,BhanuPrakash ,Prakasha SP in Kolar a small town in South Karnataka with a single BUS and a vision that was way ahead of its time. SBP gradually expanded its services to Bangalore, Mysore and Hubli. From this Humble brginning SBP has today grown into a nationally renowned logistics and travel company which is also currentyl the largest fleet owner of commercial vehicles in India with a fleet of 4866 vechicles.
